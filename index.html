About Me!

My name is Sally Blodgett and I am a junior at University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am studying Journalism, focusing on strategic communication, in the School of Journalism and Mass Communication in the College of Letters & Sciences, as well as pursuing a certificate in Digital Studies. On campus, I am the treasurer and co-captain of the club field hockey team and a member of the UW-Madison chapter of the Public Relations Student Society of America. In the summers I work in a restaurant and fish market on Cape Cod in Massachussetts.
